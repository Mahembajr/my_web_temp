IOT Club

Connecting Devices, Empowering People.

An IoT Club is like a geeky hangout for people who love gadgets and making things talk to the internet! You learn about cool stuff: sensors, lights, apps, and how to connect them. You build things: like smart lamps or automatic plant waterers. You meet new people: other gadget lovers and maybe even future employers! It's all about fun and learning!
